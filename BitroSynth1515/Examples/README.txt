BitroSynth1515 v2.0.0 - Example Sounds

This folder contains 3 example sounds.
The examples are simple and are provided only to
demonstrate the basic waveforms of the synthesizer.
They are not produced by a professional music producer.

Usage:
- You can listen to these files directly or use them as reference in your projects.
- For more advanced music production, you can combine them with other DAWs or audio editors.

Thank you and enjoy!
